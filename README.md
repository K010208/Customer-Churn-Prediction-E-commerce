# 📊 Amazon Sales Executive Dashboard & Revenue Risk Analytics

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project solves a real-world **e-commerce business analytics problem** inspired by Amazon / Flipkart-style retail operations.

The objective was to analyze sales performance, identify revenue leakage, highlight at-risk segments, and provide business recommendations through an executive dashboard.

This project demonstrates **Business Analyst + Data Analytics + Machine Learning** skills with strong business storytelling.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
E-commerce companies often face:
- inconsistent sales across categories
- region-wise underperformance
- revenue leakage due to excessive discounts
- lower customer satisfaction impacting future sales
- inefficient payment conversion

The goal of this project is to identify these drivers and support **data-driven business decisions**.

---

## 📂 Dataset Features
The dataset contains the following fields:

- `order_id`
- `order_date`
- `product_id`
- `product_category`
- `price`
- `discount_percent`
- `quantity_sold`
- `customer_region`
- `payment_method`
- `rating`
- `review_count`
- `discounted_price`
- `total_revenue`

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
The executive dashboard includes:

- 💰 Total Revenue
- ⚠️ Revenue at Risk
- ⭐ Average Rating
- 🏷️ Average Discount %
- 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend
- 🛍️ Revenue by Category
- 🌍 Revenue by Region
- 💳 Payment Revenue Share

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
- Electronics and Fashion categories contributed the highest revenue share
- Certain regions showed lower sales performance and growth opportunity
- Higher discounts indicated possible margin leakage
- Low-rated products showed future revenue risk
- Payment method preferences influenced total sales

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategy for low-margin categories
- Improve customer experience for low-rated products
- Run region-specific promotional campaigns
- Focus on prepaid payment incentives
- Improve inventory planning for high-performing categories
